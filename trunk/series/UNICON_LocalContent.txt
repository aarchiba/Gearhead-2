		STC DISPLAYCASE-1

		% V1 = Lock Picked

		GoAlarm <Print 5 TeamAttack 3 PCFEnemy 3 AddRenown -10 AddLawful -5>
		GoUnlocked <Print 4>
		GoCBFail <Print 2>

		Msg2 <You can't disarm this lock at your current skill level.>
