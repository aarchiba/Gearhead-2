

Plot
	name <Target Has Moved On>
	requires <#L +Tht T:++>
	desc <The PC will be told that his target has moved on, but won't be told where.>

	% E1 is the informant
	% E2 is the new city
	% E3 is the target
	Element1 <Character !Near -7 Mechanic>
	Element2 <Scene !Far -7 !Comrade -7 Town>
	Element3 <Grab 5>

	sub
		Persona 1
		rumor <%name1% might know where %name3% went.>
		*greeting <*LookingForNPC %3% GoSuccess GoFail>
		GoSuccess <NewChat Say 1 StoryNote 2   LoseEpisode %2% .next>
		GoFail <NewChat Say 3 StoryNote 2   LoseEpisode %2% .next>
		.next <>
		Msg1 <Yeah, %name3% was in here a while back to get \PPR %3% mecha repaired. As far as I know \SPR %3% 's left town now, but I don't know where \SPR %3% went.>
		Msg2 <%name1% told you that %name3% has left town.>
		Msg3 <I don't know anything about %name3% . As far as I know, \SPR %3% 's not even in town anymore.>
	end
